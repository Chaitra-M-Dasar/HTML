My Favorite Movies

  1. KGF
    • Year: 2018
    • Main Actors: Yash, Srinidhi Shetty
    • Description: KGF follows the rise of a young man, Rocky, who aims to gain power and wealth in the gold mines of Kolar, India. The film portrays his struggles and battles as he rises to prominence.
  2. Googly
    • Year: 2013
    • Main Actors: Yash
    • Description: Googly is a romantic comedy that revolves around the love story of a charming young man and a girl he meets during his college years. The film explores their journey and the challenges they face together.
  3. RRR
    • Year: 2022
    • Main Actors: Ram Charan, N.T. Rama Rao Jr., Alia Bhatt
    • Description: RRR is a fictional tale set in the 1920s about two Indian revolutionaries who fight against British colonial rule. The film combines intense action sequences with a gripping narrative of friendship and patriotism.
  4. Bahubali
    • Year: 2017
    • Main Actors: Prabhas, Anushka Shetty
    • Description: Bahubali is a historical epic that tells the story of a young prince who discovers his royal heritage and leads his kingdom against a tyrannical ruler. The film is known for its grand visuals and epic storytelling.
  5. KGF Chapter 2
    • Year: 2022
    • Main Actors: Yash, Srinidhi Shetty
    • Description: The sequel to KGF, KGF Chapter 2 continues the story of Rocky as he consolidates his power in the gold mines and faces new adversaries. The film expands on the epic saga with more action and drama.
